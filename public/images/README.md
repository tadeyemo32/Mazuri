# Site images

Drop your own 4K oil/energy images here to replace the defaults:

- `hero.jpg` – Hero background (e.g. offshore rig, refinery) – recommended 2560×1440 or larger
- `operations.jpg` – Operations section (e.g. refinery, pipeline) – 1920×1080+
- `goal.jpg` – Our goal section (e.g. field, facility) – 1920×1080+
- `philosophy.jpg` – Corporate philosophy (e.g. facility, team) – 1920×1080+

Use JPG or WebP. The site will use these if present; otherwise it falls back to high-quality stock imagery.
